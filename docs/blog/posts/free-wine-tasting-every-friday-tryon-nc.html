Free Wine Tasting Every Friday in Tryon

Every Friday, we pour. No reservation, no wine degree, no pressure. Here's why it might be the easiest plan you make all week.

Most people think a wine tasting is a stuffy thing. White tablecloths, a guy swirling and frowning, and a quiz at the end you didn't study for. That's not what happens here.

Every Friday at The Tryon Bottle, we set out a few bottles and pour them for free. You walk in, you try something, you chat. That's the whole event. Last Friday drew a busy, happy crowd, and not one person had to know the difference between a Pinot Grigio and a Pinot Noir to have a good time.

How the Friday tasting works

It's about as low-key as it gets. You don't need to call ahead or buy anything. Here's the short version:

  • Come by on any Friday. Walk in, no reservation needed.
  • We'll have a handful of wines open and ready to pour.
  • Tell us what you usually like, or tell us nothing, and we'll hand you something worth trying.
  • Like it? We've got the bottle on the shelf. Don't? Try the next one. No pressure either way.

You don't need to "know wine"

This is the part we want you to hear. The whole point of a tasting is to find out what you like, and the only way to do that is to taste. We're not here to test you. We're here to point you toward a bottle you'll actually enjoy, whether that's a $14 weeknight red or something a little fancier for the weekend.

Ask us anything. "What goes with tacos?" is a real question we love answering, especially since Della Street Tacos is serving right here in the shop.

Make it your standing Friday plan

Downtown Tryon is worth a walk, and Trade Street is the heart of it. A free tasting is a pretty good excuse to park the car, stretch your legs, and end up somewhere with a cold drink and a patio seat. We're open and pouring every Friday, so there's always a reason to come find us.

A curated small shop beats the big box every time. You get a real recommendation, a cold drink, and a place to actually stay a while.

Bring a friend, bring your curiosity, and we'll handle the rest. See you Friday.
